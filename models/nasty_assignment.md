# Assigning people to jobs

We have 4 people and 4 jobs. Each person can do any job but the costs differ.

Person 1 costs: 9, 2, 7, 8
Person 2 costs: 6, 4, 3, 7
Person 3 costs: 5, 8, 1, 8
Person 4 costs: 7, 6, 9, 4

Find the cheapest assignment where every person does exactly one job
and every job is done by exactly one person. (This is a 0-1 problem.)
